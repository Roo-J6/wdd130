Akwa Ibom

Akwa Ibom State is a coastal state located in the South-South geopolitical zone of Nigeria. Created in 1987, it is one of the 36 states in the country. Akwa Ibom is rich in natural resources, including crude oil, natural gas, and limestone. The state is also known for its agricultural produce, such as cassava, yams, and palm oil.
